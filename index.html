About Us

Hole in one is a golf resort History

Hole in One Golf Resort is a premier golf destination in Portugal, offering a world-class experience for golf enthusiasts. Our resort features three championship golf courses, including the renowned Sea View Course, known for its breathtaking coastal scenery. Guests can enjoy top-tier facilities, including two clubhouses, a luxury hotel, fine dining restaurants, and a café. Our Pro Shop provides the latest golf equipment and apparel. Beyond the greens, we offer a floodlit driving range, expert golf lessons, digital coaching, and a professional caddie service to enhance your game. Whether you're a seasoned golfer or a beginner, Hole in One Golf Resort ensures an unforgettable experience in a stunning setting.
